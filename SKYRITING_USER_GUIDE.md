## Introduction

Skyriting is a premium private aviation platform that connects you with luxury aircraft for your travel needs. Whether you need a one-way trip, round-trip, or multi-city journey, Skyriting makes private aviation accessible and transparent.

### What You Can Do
- **Search and Book Flights**: Find available aircraft for your route
- **View Aircraft Details**: See specifications, photos, and pricing
- **Manage Bookings**: View your bookings, request reschedules
- **Join Mobility Thread**: Share experiences and connect with the community
- **Read News & Articles**: Stay updated with aviation news

## How to Book a Flight

### Step 1: Search for Flights

1. **Go to the Home Page**
   - Visit `https://skyriting.com` (or your domain)
   - You'll see the search widget at the top

2. **Select Trip Type**
   - **One-Way**: Single flight from origin to destination
   - **Round-Trip**: Flight to destination and back to origin
   - **Multi-Trip**: Multiple legs (e.g., City A → City B → City C → City A)

3. **Enter Flight Details**
   - **Departure City**: Select from dropdown (cities managed by admin)
   - **Arrival City**: Select from dropdown
   - **Departure Date**: Choose your travel date
   - **Departure Time**: Select preferred time
   - **Passengers**: Number of passengers (1-20)
   - **For Round-Trip**: Also enter return date and time
   - **For Multi-Trip**: Click "Add Leg" to add more destinations

4. **Click "Search Flights"**
   - System searches for available aircraft matching your criteria

### Step 2: View Search Results

You'll see a list of available aircraft with:
- **Aircraft Name & Type**: e.g., "Cessna Citation CJ2 - Light Jet"
- **Capacity**: Number of seats
- **Pricing Breakdown**:
  - Flight Cost
  - Airport Handling
  - GST (18%)
  - **Estimated Total Cost**

**Filters Available:**
- Aircraft Class (Light, Mid, Super Mid, Large, Airliner, Helicopter)
- Minimum Capacity
- Price Range
- Amenities (WiFi, Entertainment, Catering, etc.)

**Sort Options:**
- By Price (Lowest to Highest)
- By Capacity
- By Speed

### Step 3: View Aircraft Details

1. **Click "View Details"** on any aircraft
2. **Aircraft Detail Page Shows:**
   - **Specifications**:
     - Seats
     - Year of Manufacture (YOM)
     - Base Location
     - Number of Pilots
     - Baggage Capacity
     - Speed (KTS)
     - Flight Attendant (Yes/No)
     - Cabin Dimensions (Height, Width, Length)
     - Lavatory Count
     - Flying Range
   - **Interior Photos**: Gallery of cabin photos
   - **Range Map**: Visual representation of flight range
   - **Flight Details**: All legs of your trip
   - **Cost Breakdown**:
     - Flight Cost
     - Airport Handling
     - GST (18%)
     - **Estimated Total Cost**

3. **Fill Out Enquiry Form**
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number (with country code)
   - Message (optional)
   - Click "Submit Enquiry"

### Step 4: Admin Creates Quote

1. **Admin Reviews Your Enquiry**
   - Admin receives your enquiry
   - Admin selects the best aircraft for your route
   - System automatically calculates pricing

2. **You Receive Quote Email**
   - Email sent to your registered email address
   - Contains:
     - Quote number
     - Aircraft details
     - Complete pricing breakdown
     - Flight itinerary
     - Quote validity period (usually 7 days)

### Step 5: Accept Quote and Complete Booking

1. **Login to Your Account**
   - Go to "User Login" in navigation
   - Enter email and password
   - If not registered, click "Sign Up"

2. **View Your Quotes**
   - Go to "Account" page
   - Click on "Quotes" section
   - Find your quote

3. **Accept Quote**
   - Review quote details
   - Click "Accept Quote"
   - Booking is created automatically

4. **Receive Booking Confirmation**
   - Email confirmation sent immediately
   - Contains:
     - Booking number
     - Flight details
     - Contact information
     - Payment status

5. **View Booking in Account**
   - Go to "Account" → "Bookings"
   - See all your bookings
   - View booking details
   - Request reschedule if needed

## Understanding Routes

### What are Routes?

Routes are predefined city pairs that Skyriting serves. Each route includes:
- **Origin Airport Code**: e.g., DEL (Delhi)
- **Destination Airport Code**: e.g., BOM (Mumbai)
- **Origin City**: e.g., New Delhi
- **Destination City**: e.g., Mumbai
- **Distance**: In kilometers
- **Estimated Time**: Flight duration in hours

### How Routes Work

1. **Admin Adds Routes**
   - Admin adds all available routes in the system
   - Routes are marked as "Popular" if frequently booked

2. **You Select Routes**
   - When searching, you select from available routes
   - Only routes added by admin appear in dropdowns

3. **Route Availability**
   - Not all city pairs may be available
   - Contact admin if you need a route not listed

### Popular Routes

Popular routes are highlighted in the search dropdown. These routes typically have:
- More frequent availability
- Better pricing options
- Faster response times

## Pricing Explained

### Transparent Cost-Plus Model

Skyriting uses a transparent pricing model. You see exactly what you're paying for:

### Pricing Formula

```
Base Cost = Hourly Operating Cost × Flight Hours
+ Fuel Surcharge (per km)
+ Airport Fees (per leg)
+ Crew Expenses (per hour)
= Subtotal

+ Ground Handling (one-time)
= Subtotal with Handling

+ Margin (based on aircraft commission or pricing rules)
= Subtotal with Margin

+ Tax (GST - 18%)
= Total Before Discount

- Multi-Leg Discount (if applicable, after 3+ legs)
= Final Total
```

### Pricing Components

1. **Hourly Operating Cost**
   - Base cost per hour of flight
   - Varies by aircraft type
   - Set by admin for each aircraft

2. **Fuel Surcharge**
   - Calculated per kilometer
   - Based on route distance
   - Set in pricing rules

3. **Airport Fees**
   - Charged per leg (each takeoff/landing)
   - Standard fee set in pricing rules

4. **Crew Expenses**
   - Calculated per hour of flight
   - Includes pilot and crew costs

5. **Ground Handling**
   - One-time fee per trip
   - Covers ground services

6. **Margin**
   - Applied to cover operational costs
   - Priority:
     1. Aircraft-specific commission (if set)
     2. Aircraft-type margin (Light, Mid, etc.)
     3. Global margin
   - Transparently shown in breakdown

7. **Tax (GST)**
   - 18% GST applied
   - Calculated on subtotal with margin

8. **Multi-Leg Discount**
   - Applied automatically for trips with 3+ legs
   - Discount percentage set by admin
   - Shown as savings in breakdown

### Example Pricing

**Trip**: Delhi (DEL) → Mumbai (BOM) - One-Way
**Aircraft**: Cessna Citation CJ2
**Distance**: 1,400 km
**Flight Time**: 3.5 hours
**Passengers**: 4

**Breakdown**:
- Base Flying Cost: ₹17,50,000 (₹5,00,000/hour × 3.5 hours)
- Fuel Surcharge: ₹1,40,000 (₹100/km × 1,400 km)
- Airport Fees: ₹50,000 (per leg)
- Crew Expenses: ₹1,75,000 (₹50,000/hour × 3.5 hours)
- **Subtotal**: ₹21,15,000
- Ground Handling: ₹1,00,000
- **Subtotal with Handling**: ₹22,15,000
- Margin (15%): ₹3,32,250
- **Subtotal with Margin**: ₹25,47,250
- GST (18%): ₹4,58,505
- **Total**: ₹30,05,755

### Factors Affecting Price

1. **Aircraft Type**
   - Light jets: Lower cost, smaller capacity
   - Large jets: Higher cost, more capacity
   - Helicopters: Different pricing structure

2. **Route Distance**
   - Longer routes = higher fuel costs
   - More flight hours = higher operating costs

3. **Trip Type**
   - One-way: Single leg pricing
   - Round-trip: Two legs (may have discount)
   - Multi-trip: Multiple legs (discount after 3+ legs)

4. **Aircraft Commission**
   - Some aircraft have specific commission rates
   - Can override standard pricing rules

5. **Time of Booking**
   - Last-minute bookings may have premium
   - Advance bookings may have discounts

## Account Management

### Creating an Account

1. **Click "User Login"** in navigation
2. **Click "Sign Up"** or "Create Account"
3. **Fill Registration Form**:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (min 8 characters)
   - Phone Number (with country code)
4. **Submit Registration**
5. **Verify Email**:
   - Check your email inbox
   - Click verification link
   - Or copy link to browser
   - Link expires in 24 hours

### Login

1. **Click "User Login"** in navigation
2. **Enter Email and Password**
3. **Click "Login"**
4. Navigation changes to "Account"

### Account Features

**Profile Management**:
- View and update profile
- Change password
- Update phone number
- Upload profile photo

**Bookings**:
- View all bookings
- See booking details
- Request reschedule

**Quotes**:
- View all quotes
- Accept quotes
- See quote details
- Track quote validity

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Booking Questions

**Q: How far in advance should I book?**
A: We recommend booking at least 7-14 days in advance for best availability and pricing. Last-minute bookings (within 48 hours) may have limited availability.

**Q: Can I cancel my booking?**
A: Yes, contact admin through your account or email. Cancellation policies apply based on timing.

**Q: Can I reschedule my flight?**
A: Yes, go to your booking in Account → Bookings → Request Reschedule. Admin will review and confirm.

**Q: What if my route is not listed?**
A: Contact admin at info@skyriting.com. We can arrange custom routes based on availability.

**Q: How many passengers can I book for?**
A: Maximum 20 passengers. Select appropriate aircraft based on capacity.

### Pricing Questions

**Q: Why does pricing vary between aircraft?**
A: Different aircraft have different operating costs, capacities, and amenities. Larger aircraft typically cost more.

**Q: Is GST included in the price?**
A: Yes, GST (18%) is included in the final price shown. Breakdown shows all components.

**Q: Do I get a discount for round-trip?**
A: Round-trip pricing is calculated for both legs. Multi-trip (3+ legs) may have discounts.

**Q: Can I negotiate the price?**
A: Pricing is transparent and calculated automatically. Contact admin for special requests or group bookings.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Payment details are provided after quote acceptance. Contact admin for payment options.

### Account Questions

**Q: Do I need to create an account to book?**
A: You can submit an enquiry without an account, but you need an account to accept quotes and manage bookings.

**Q: I didn't receive verification email. What should I do?**
A: Check spam folder. Use "Resend Verification Email" in your account. Wait 60 seconds between requests.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Contact admin at info@skyriting.com to change your email address.

**Q: How do I update my profile?**
A: Login → Account → Profile section → Update details → Save.

### Technical Questions

**Q: The website is not loading. What should I do?**
A: Check your internet connection. Clear browser cache. Try a different browser. Contact support if issue persists.

**Q: I can't see search results. Why?**
A: Ensure you've selected valid routes and dates. Some routes may not have available aircraft. Try different dates or contact admin.

**Q: Images are not loading.**
A: Check your internet connection. Some images may take time to load. Refresh the page.

### General Questions

**Q: What makes Skyriting different?**
A: Transparent pricing, comprehensive aircraft details, easy booking process, and community features.

**Q: Is Skyriting available worldwide?**
A: Currently focused on specific regions. Check available routes or contact admin for international flights.

**Q: How do I contact support?**
A: Email: info@skyriting.com
   Phone: Check Contact Us page
   Account: Use messaging in your account

**Q: Can I book for someone else?**
A: Yes, you can book for others. Provide their details in the enquiry form or booking.
